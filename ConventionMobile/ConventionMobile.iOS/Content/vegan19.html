Vegan:

Places with many vegan options:

  • Three Carrots $ (6 blocks away, Inside City Market @ 222 E Market St)

    • Vegan/Veggie cuisine only, full meals

  • Greenleaf $ (3 blocks away, south end of Monument Circle on S Meridian St)

    • Salads, veggie bowls, juices

  • Nada $ (1 block away in circle center mall)

    • Fried avocado tacos

    • Black bean tacos

  • Chipotle Mexican Grill $ (3 blocks away, NE corner of E Washington and N Meridian)

    • soft & hard tortillas, white & brown rice, black & pinto beans, fajita vegetables, sofritas, all salsas, chips, and guacamole

  • Mikado $$ (1 block away, NW Corner of W Georgia St and Illinois St)

    • Has 6 vegan rolls (make sure to ask no for no fish sauces)

    • Edamame

    • Sauteed Mushroom entree (ask if it's possible to make without butter/fish sauce?)

    • Salads, Miso Soup

  • Yard House $$ (1 1/2 blocks away, S side of W Maryland St)

    • Guacamole & Chips (ask for no feta)

    • Shiitake & Crimini Mushrooms Lettuce Wrap

    • Hummus

    • Chicken Nachos (sub Gardein chicken, no cheese, no enchilada sauce, no sour cream)

    • Crispy Brussels Sprouts & Ripped Potatoes (ask for no malt vinegar aioli)

    • Hot & Spicy or Chilled Edamame

    • Sweet Potato or Truffle Fries (ask for no maple bacon cream dip, no parmesan)

    • Gardein Buffalo Wings (ask for no ranch, or bring your own vegan ranch)

    • Vegan Burger (comes with Daiya cheese!)

    • Gardein Blackened Chicken Torta (ask for Daiya cheese instead of dairy, no chipotle mayo, no cumin crema)

    • Gardein Grilled Chicken & Avocado Sandwich (ask for Daiya cheese instead of dairy, no mayo)

    • Angel Hair Pasta (ask for no feta)

    • Gardein Chicken Rice Bowl

    • Summer Salad

    • Kale Caesar Salad (ask for no parmesan, sub oil and vinegar instead of caesar dressing)

    • Chopped Salad (ask for no bacon)

    • Mango Sorbet

  • Whole Foods Daily Grill $ (7 blocks away NE corner of E Market St and N Alabama St)

    • Beyond the Meat Vegan Burger

    • Fried Cauliflower plate

    • Veggie Dumplings

    • Broccoli Nuggets

    • Black Bean Burger

    • Beer Battered Tofu and Chips w/vegan mayo dip

  • Cafe Patachou $$ – breakfast food only (1 block away, SW corner of W Washington & S Capitol Ave)

    • Vegan Cuban Breakfast

    • Vegan Omelette

    • Oatmeal, Porridge, Quinoa + Oatmeal, Granola, Bagels

    • Hearthy Grain Bowl w/Mushrooms & Kale

    • Avocado Multigrain Toast

    • Fruit Salad


Places with a few vegan options:

  • Burger Study $$ - 1 block away, in circle center mall – has Beyond Meat patties for any burger (ask for no cheese, mayo, butter). Also has fried avocado wedge appetizer, ask to make sure no eggs. Ask about fryer oil, unsure

  • Rock Bottom Brewery $$ - 3 blocks away, NW corner of W Washington St and S Meridian St – has veggie burger (per nutrition has no dairy or eggs) and veggie tacos, ask for no cheese mayo or butter. Ask about fries, unsure

  • Primanti Brothers $$ - 1 block away, SE corner of W Maryland St and Illinois St – has Southwest Black Bean Burger (it has no eggs or dairy, per staff answer), fries are vegan but onion rings are NOT, ask for no cheese, no mayo, no butter

  • Kilroy's $$ - 3 blocks away, SW corner of E Maryland St and S Pennsylvania St – has portabella cap burger, ask for no cheese, no mayo, no butter

  • Spicebox $ – 6 blocks away or delivers on grubhub – has Channa Tandori Taco or Aloo Spice Wrap, ask about others

  • The Crowne Plaza restaurant $$ – has a vegan pasta dish, ask

  • The RAM $$ - 1 block away on Illinois St, between W Marilyand and W Georgia - has the impossible burger, but no dairy-free cheeses, fries maybe? (ask if they fry in vegetable oil without beef tallow)

  • Punch Bowl Social $$ - 2 blocks away, NW corner of Georgia & S Meridian – has the impossible burger, but no dairy-free cheeses, some hummus, fries maybe? (ask if they fry in vegetable oil without beef tallow)

  • Scotty's Brewhouse $$ - 3 blocks away, NE corner of E Maryland and Pennsylvania – Salads, Falafel balls, fries maybe? (ask if they fry in vegetable oil without beef tallow)

  • The Flying Cupcake $ – If they have their food truck, ask if they have vegan cupcakes available
